Instructions

  1. There are two players each player having a health of 100.
  2. Players can shoot at each other with a random power (0-5) which will reduce the opponent's health.
  3. The Player whose health reaches 0, dies and the game ends.
  4. There are 5 such rounds, the player with the most game wins, wins the match/tournament.

Player 1 press right arrow (->) key and Player 2 press left arrow(<-) key to fire.
